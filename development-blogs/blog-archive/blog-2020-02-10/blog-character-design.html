Character Design and How it Changes my Website

Posted by Alex Carvalho on the 10th February 2020

I wanted to design my web page as the front page of an influencer. Visual imagery is the primary focus for most posts considering that my character is intended to be social media sites that I have visited. Each post consisting of a title followed by an image with a description attached to it. However, since my character does not have access to a camera and therefore relies on stock images, the Image will be an accessory to the post as a form of Visual/iconic/metaphoric representation of what is being posted. My intention is to shift the 'viewers' perspective from 'This happened' to 'Something like this happened' as the visual 'proof' is just a representation of the 'truth.'
