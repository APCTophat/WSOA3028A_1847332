Design Research and Shape Theory

Posted by Alex Carvalho on the 3rd February 2020

Whilst looking at websites to get ideas for how I could design my website, I came across the website called SoInteractive. What struck me the most about this website was its colour and shape identity. I looked at "The Meaning of Shapes: Developing visual Grammar" gain a better understanding of shape theory. I decided that I wanted a triangular theme to run through my webpage. To enforce that my character is stable and reliable. This shape theme will be incorporated in buttons, tabs or image outlines.
