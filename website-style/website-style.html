This is a shape

I consider a style for a website to be the shapes/colours and layout which are presented to the viewer. This is still an area I am learning about, but I know certain things that I want to do with the website.

Colour

I want to give my website a strong colour identity but not have it be gaudy. Looking at other social media sites such as WhatsApp, Instagram and Twitter I have noted certain aspects of their colour usage:

  • They tend to stay within one colour, with some using gradients of a major colour for clarity between different elements on the site
  • Most of the screen is taken up by a white base with black text or vice versa for ‘night’ modes.
  • Colour is generally used for buttons/ profile bars and a general highlight tool. To generally say that “this thing” is important to the viewer

    • So I want to use colour in a similar manor on my website. Firstly I need a colour and I think I will be going for these ones:

    I personally like blue and I wanted an soft colour that would blend in nicely into a background. So I decided to go with #89CFF0 with a monochromatic partner #5CBDEA. I decided to have a monochromatic partner to provide more definition/variety on the website. I decided on these two colours using thisColour Wheel website and using “Baby Blue” as my base colour.

    Note: This is still subject to change and completely depends on how the development or personal preferences change over the course of the website.
