Design Reasearch and Shape Theory


                    Whilst looking at websites to get ideas for how I could design my website,
                    I came across the website called SoInteractive.
                    What struck me the most about this website was its colour and shape identity.
                    I looked at  "The Meaning of Shapes: Developing visual Grammar" gain
                    a better understanding of shape theory. I decided that I wanted a triangular theme 
                    to run through my webpage. To enforce that my character is stable and reliable.
                    This shape theme will be encorparated in buttons, tabs or image outlines.

Character Design and How it Changes My Website


                    When considering that my character is intended to be social media influencer.
                    I wanted to design my web page as the front page of an influencer. Visual Imagry
                    is the primary focus for most social media sites that I have visited. Each post 
                    consisting of a title followed by an image with a description attached to it.
                
                    However, since my character does not have access to a camera and therefore relies
                    on stock images, the Image will beign an accessory to the post as a form of
                    Visual/iconic/metaphoric representation of what is being posted. My intention
                    is to shift the 'viewers' perspective from 'This happened' to 'Something like this happend'
                    as the visual 'proof' is just a representaion of the 'truth.'
